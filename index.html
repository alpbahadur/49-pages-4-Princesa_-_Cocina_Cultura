Our Story

Nestled along the historic Paseo de la Princesa in Old San Juan, Princesa - Cocina Cultura offers a unique dining experience that transcends time. We are the only restaurant specializing in recipes from 19th-century Puerto Rican cookbooks (1859-1950), bringing history to life on every plate.

Our enchanting garden setting, embraced by over 20 trees and bonded to a historic wall, creates a magical atmosphere where past and present merge. Each dish tells a story of Puerto Rican heritage, crafted with passion and presented with elegance.

The Rum Library

Discover one of the most extensive collections of Puerto Rican rum in the world. Our curated selection spans centuries of distilling tradition, from rare vintage bottles to contemporary craft expressions.

Each rum tells a story of the island's rich heritage. Our knowledgeable staff will guide you through tastings and pairings that elevate your dining experience to new heights.
